Business 2: Travel agency
Pain points:
After-hours missed leads - messages come in late night, no one responds until morning.
Employees receive inquiries but give poor consulting and don't follow up properly.
Low conversion rate - 1000 customers in, only 100 sold (target is 300-400).
Manual pricing calculations in Excel lead to errors - complex tiers for hotels, room types, guest counts, surcharges.
Employee quality control - hard to monitor if staff meet customer service standards.
No reporting system - no revenue reports by date, hotel, salesperson, or source.
